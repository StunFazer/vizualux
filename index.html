Setup & Calibration Guide

Get your interactive floor projection mapped and calibrated in 4 quick steps.

1

Select Camera

Grant webcam permissions and choose your input device from the Camera Source dropdown inside the control deck.

2

Warp & Calibrate

Click Open Projector. Enable Test Screen to see color corner targets, and drag the 4 corner points to map the camera view to the projection area. Use Arrow keys to nudge the selected point, hold Shift for larger steps.

3

Set Background

Ensure the interactive floor projection area is completely clear of people or moving objects, then click Set Reference.

4

Interact & Play

Choose an interactive effect or game from Active Content. Walk or step on the projected area to trigger physics and visual effects!
